Examples (Headshots)

Headshots are usually drawn from the neck or bust upwards, whether or not I draw from the bust or neck up depends on the character. I'll usually draw the bust when the character has clothing or important accessories.



Examples (Halfbody)

A halfbody is drawn from the waist/hips upwards, depending on the pose of the character. Poses can be complex or simple.



Examples (Fullbody)

A fullbody is, as the name implies, a drawing of the entire character. Poses can be complex or simple.
