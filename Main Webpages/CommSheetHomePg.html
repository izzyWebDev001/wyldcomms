Terms of Service

  • You cannot remove my watermark from my artwork.
  • I will send you a sketch, and if there are no changes you would like me to make, then you are to proceed with payment.
  • I will not proceed with the commission until paid.
  • I have the right to refuse a commission if it makes me uncomfortable.
  • I will require a digital reference sheet, or in the very least, a digital colour palette in order to do your commission.
  • The above rule this does not apply to those who are commissioning me for a reference sheet.
  • Please do not resell lineart I have made for you as a base or ych.
  • Payment in paypal only, I accept DA points from friends/mutuals.
  • Refunds are allowed until one stage before your commission request (for example, if you ordered a shaded commission, you are allowed to refund up until the flats stage. Or, if you ordered a flat-colour commission, you can only refund up until the lineart stage).
  • If you get a refund, I will likely repurpose the commission into a ych or a base.
  • To commission suggestive content you must be 18 or older.
  • Additional characters cost an extra 50% of the original price.
